> **Last Updated:** 2026-06-08
> **Duration:** 30 seconds

## The Pitch

> Every year, millions of students lose their futures to paper leaks and exam fraud. Current systems fail because they trust too much — central servers, network connections, human processes.
>
> **FortisExam is a Zero-Trust Examination Infrastructure** that makes paper leaks mathematically impossible, copying between candidates useless, and every exam action cryptographically accountable.
>
> Questions are encrypted until exam time. Adjacent candidates see completely different papers. Every action is logged in a tamper-evident ledger. And the entire exam runs offline — no internet, no cloud bottleneck, no single point of failure.
>
> **We don't monitor students. We eliminate the vectors of fraud.**

---

## Key Differentiators (3 bullets)

1. **Not proctoring — infrastructure.** We solve leaks and accountability, not just surveillance.
2. **Offline-first edge computing.** Each exam center operates independently. Network outages don't cancel exams.
3. **Cryptographic guarantees.** Security is mathematical, not procedural. Tampering is detectable, not just discouraged.
